# Editing the Menu via Gemini AI (No Coding)

There are two ways you can use Gemini AI to manage your website menu sheet using natural language. 

---

## 🌟 Method A: Using the built-in Gemini panel inside Google Sheets (Recommended)
Since you are already inside the spreadsheet, this method is the most direct, fast, and reliable.

1. Open your **"Goutam Krishna Sweets"** Google Sheet.
2. Click the **Gemini button** (the spark/star icon in the top right toolbar of Google Sheets) to open the side panel.
3. Type your instructions directly in the side panel. For example:
   * `"Change the price of Black Forest to 800"`
   * `"Change the availability of Mysore Pak to NO"`
   * `"Set all cake prices that are currently empty to Enquire"`
   * `"Add a new row for Dry Fruit Laddu with price 250"`
4. Review the change and press **Enter** or click **Apply** if prompted.

---

## 💬 Method B: Using the Gemini Chat App (gemini.google.com)
If you are on your phone or not currently inside Google Sheets, you can use the main Gemini Chat App.

### ⚙️ How to Enable Google Drive in Gemini:
1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com).
2. Click on **Extensions** (puzzle piece icon in the bottom-left menu).
3. Toggle the **Google Workspace** extension to **ON**.
4. Confirm permissions to allow Gemini to access your Google Drive.

### 💬 Prompts to type:
Make sure to specify the exact name of the sheet: **"Goutam Krishna Sweets"**.
* `"Open my Google Sheet named 'Goutam Krishna Sweets' and change the price of Black Forest to 800."`
* `"Open my Google Sheet named 'Goutam Krishna Sweets' and change the 'available' status of Mysore Pak to NO."`

---

## 💡 Important Tips
* **Google Account**: Make sure you are logged into the Gemini Chat App with the **same Google Account** that owns the Google Sheet.
* **Republish Delay**: After Gemini makes changes to your sheet, Google takes **2 to 3 minutes** to update the published website link. Wait a couple of minutes before refreshing your site to see the new prices!
