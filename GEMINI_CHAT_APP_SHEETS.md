# Editing the Menu via the Gemini Chat App (No Coding)

You can use the consumer **Gemini Chat App** (gemini.google.com or the Gemini app on Android/iOS) to edit your website menu spreadsheet using natural language.

---

## ⚙️ How to Enable Google Workspace inside Gemini

1. Go to [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com).
2. Click on **Extensions** (puzzle piece icon in the bottom-left menu).
3. Toggle the **Google Workspace** extension to **ON**.
4. Confirm permissions to allow Gemini to view and modify files in your Google Drive.

---

## 💬 Prompts the Owner Can Use

Open the Gemini Chat App and type any of these prompts. Make sure to specify the exact name of the sheet: **"Goutam Krishna Sweets"**.

### 1. To Change Prices
> "Open my Google Sheet named 'Goutam Krishna Sweets' and change the price of Black Forest to 600."

### 2. To Hide Out of Stock Items
> "Open my Google Sheet named 'Goutam Krishna Sweets' and change the 'available' status of Mysore Pak to NO."

### 3. To Auto-Fill Missing Fields (Translations & Categories)
> "Open my Google Sheet named 'Goutam Krishna Sweets'. Find any new rows that are missing alt names or categories. Automatically fill them in: translate the name to Malayalam in the alt column, classify the category, and set the image_key to the closest matching item from the existing list."

### 4. To Add a New Product
> "Open my Google Sheet named 'Goutam Krishna Sweets' and add a new sweet called 'Dry Fruit Laddu'. Set alt to 'ഡ്രൈ ഫ്രൂട്ട് ലഡ്ഡു', type to 'sweet', category to 'laddu', price to '250', image_key to 'datesladdu', and available to 'YES'."

---

## 💡 Pro Tips for Best Results
* **Name matches:** Make sure you refer to product names exactly as they are spelt in the sheet (e.g. use "Kaju Katli", not "kajukutly").
* **Drive Account:** Ensure you are logged into the Gemini Chat App with the **same Google Account** that owns the Google Sheet in Google Drive.
